Fried Rice

What is Fried Rice?

Fried rice is Chinese is origin, but at it's core is just rice cooked in aromatics and fried

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil, or to taste

Directions

  1. Assemble ingredients
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a slow boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Sir in peas, and immediately drain in a colander
