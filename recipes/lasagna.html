Lasagna

Making the World's Best Lasagna

Lasagna takes a little while to make, but the end result is delicious pasta
Below is a list of ingredients and step-by-step instructions xdd

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor
  • Tomato products: Can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles:>Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta
  • Egg: An egg helps bind ricotta so it doesn't ooze when the lasagna is cut

Making Lasagna Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
